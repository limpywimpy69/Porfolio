My Favourite Websites

Here are some of my favourite websites I visit everyday!

  • Youtube

    YouTube is a video sharing platform where users can watch, share, like, comment on videos uploaded by other users. You can also create and upload your own videos to share with others.

  • Cookie Clicker

    A game where the player initially clicks the big cookie, earning a single cookie per click. The player can now use the earned cookies to buy upgrades and assests that improve the efficiency of getting more cookies.

  • LifeAt

    LifeAt is a digital workplace environment designed to enhance productivity and focus. I use it often to study and work on projects. It allows you to set timers, select workspaces, listen to music, manage tasks, etc.

  • RPGamers Network

    RPGamers Network Radio is an online radio station that focuses on playing game music, particularly from role-playing games (RPGs). It streams game soundtracks 24/7, allowing users to listen to music from their favorite games, and also offers song requests and other interactive features

  • itch.io

    Itch.io is a website for users to host, sell and download indie video games, indie role-playing games, game assets, comics, zines and music.
